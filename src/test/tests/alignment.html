Alignment

Add classes to align elements to the start or end of a row as well as the top, bottom, or center of a column

._start

<div class="grid _start">
	<div class="col-6">
		<div class="box"></div>
	</div>
</div>

._center

<div class="grid _center">
	<div class="col-6">
		<div class="box"></div>
	</div>
</div>

._end

<div class="grid _end">
	<div class="col-6">
		<div class="box"></div>
	</div>
</div>

Here is an example of using the modifiers in conjunction to acheive different alignment at different viewport sizes.

Example 
<div class="grid _center _end-sm _start-lg">
	<div class="col-6">
		<div class="box"></div>
	</div>
</div>
